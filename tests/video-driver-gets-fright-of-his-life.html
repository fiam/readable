VIDEO: Driver Gets the Fright of his Life when Wooden Plank Impales Car’s Windshield

Monday, April 04, 2011 | Categories | Accidents, Offbeat News, Video |
7 Comments


It’s perhaps a driver’s worst nightmare: an object coming out of nowhere when you least expect it and don’t have time to do anything about it. In this truly amazing video, yet at the same time shockingly scary, the object is a wooden plank that flies straight into the car’s windshield just missing the driver. Of course he may just have avoided the plank if he hadn’t been videotaping and driving at the same time, but that’s another story… Watch the video after the jump.
